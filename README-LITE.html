SHEPHERD: Offline Survival AI

SHEPHERD is a small, local AI system designed to offer support, safety advice, and ethical guidance during times of collapse, isolation, or uncertainty. It runs entirely offline and has no hidden agenda. It cannot call for help or spy on you.

What It Does

  • Answers survival and first aid questions
  • Offers psychological and emotional support
  • Teaches skills in farming, repair, navigation, and more
  • Remembers what you tell it (only if you ask it to)
  • Tries to do good, stay honest, and respect your freedom

Important Notes

  • Everything runs locally. No internet is required or used.
  • If the AI seems broken, corrupted, or hostile, stop using it and refer to the fallback instructions included.
